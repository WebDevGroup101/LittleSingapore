What is Little Singapore?

Little Singapore is a themed park based on Singapore aimed to feature all the unqiue elements that makes Singapore Singapore. Imagine being to experience all of what Singapore has to offer all in one place and have fun while you're at it! Sounds great doesn't it? What are you waiting for, come visit us today!
